Who Am I?

Law Offices of Pamela E. Havird, Attorney at Law Business Litigation and General Practice Professional

Serving the communities of La Jolla and Southern California. Schedule an appointment Today!

Pamela E. Havird, Attorney at Law, provides service in the San Diego area for the business community, and individuals in most types of legal matters. Ms. Havird has been practicing law for more than thirty (30) years with an emphasis on business litigation. Ms. Havird is a sole practitioner, and trial lawyer with the LAW OFFICES OF PAMELA E. HAVIRD and is licensed in California and Georgia. During Ms. Havird’s career she has worked with small businesses in setting up companies, employment issues, intellectual property issues, land-lord tenant issues, enforcement of contractual disputes, and general legal issues related to the operations of businesses. Prior to practicing law, Ms. Havird worked as a Legislative Assistant in the Virginia State Legislature, and did fund raising and press releases for the former Lt. Governor of Virginia, Dick Davis, and fund raising for the former Member of the U.S. House of Representatives, Paul Simon.

We solve problems for our clients through personal relationships, and hard work. Preventative action in building your business or protecting your personal assets will create security for you and your family and give you confidence in making the best choices for enjoying your life. Our office has long term relationships with clients and is committed to helping you achieve your expectations for problem resolution and long term planning. An initial consultation can be arranged during or after business hours.

We do cross border issues between the US and Mexico and have represented many foreign nationals. In the past Pamela has worked with attorneys in Mexico City on issues arising out of NAFTA, and is now a candidate with the Law Society to practice as a British Solicitor with international clients in the States and abroad.

Our Services
